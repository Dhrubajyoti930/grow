The Supersonic Whip

A bullwhip is not merely a tool; it is a masterpiece of kinetic energy transfer. Its crack is the first human-made sound to break the sound barrier.

[ KINETIC ENERGY EQUATION ]
E = ½mv²
The whip acts as a tapering waveguide. As energy travels from the thick handle (high mass) to the thin lash (low mass), the velocity must increase to conserve momentum.

As the whip is flicked, a loop—the "wave"—travels down the leather length. Because the mass of the whip decreases exponentially toward the tip, the speed of that wave accelerates until it exceeds 343 meters per second.
